Detailed Description

View that displays a traditional piano style keyboard. Finger presses are reported to a MusicKeyListener. Keys that pressed are highlighted. Running a finger along the top of the keyboard will only hit black keys. Running a finger along the bottom of the keyboard will only hit white keys.
